Programme

The workshop will mainly consist of two kinds of talk:

  • Overview Talk. The speaker is encouraged to ruminate on the future for probabilistic numerical methods and to propose some topics for collaborative research at the workshop.
  • Research Talk. The speaker is invited to present and discuss a probabilistic numerical method. These talks will mainly focus only on very recent research, e.g. 2018+

In addition, a number of expert “discussion leaders” will steer sessions of Collaborative Research throughout the workshop.
