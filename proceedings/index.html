Proceedings

The participants of the Prob Num 2018 workshop are invited to submit research that was either presented at the workshop or discussed in the collaborative sessions to a special edition of the Springer journal Statistics and Computing.

The special edition will be edited by Mark Girolami, Ilse Ipsen, Chris Oates, Art Owen and Tim Sullivan.

Style and Templates

LaTeX template, with acknowledgement text included: LaTeX.zip.

Author style guide for the journal: [link]

In addition to the usual journal requirements, we strongly encourage submissions to aim for a length of 10–15 pages in total (excluding references and electronic supplement) in order to facilitate a timely review period.

Submission and Review Process

Deadline for submission: 3rd September 2018, 25:59 GMT.

It is a strict requirement of these proceedings that manuscripts are not accepted for publication elsewhere at the time of submission. Please note that individual journals will have their own policies on whether manuscripts can be submitted that have already appeared in the proceedings of a workshops.

Submissions and any questions should be emailed in pdf format to probnum2018@turing.ac.uk.

All submissions will be subject to peer review and a decision of accept/minor revisions/major revisions/reject will be returned. To ensure timely publication of the proceedings, any revision period will be time-limited.
